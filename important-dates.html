Graduate Fee Payment Due Dates

The payment due dates for the graduate fees are the 21st of each month during the semester.

To enroll in the payment plan for 3 payments, sign into your Student Center. Once in, find the drop-down box in the Finances tab and select "Enroll in Payment Plan".

To enroll in the payment plan for 4 payments, sign into the Graduate Portal and select "Enroll in Graduate Payment Plan".

Graduate Fee Waiver

To waive the Well-Being ($45) and Legal ($15) fees, fill out the waiver under the "Finances" tab on the Student Center.
